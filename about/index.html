Our Process

01

Conception

Planning and development, sometimes called project conception, is the beginning of the construction process. This is when a client dreams about the building or facility they desire.

02

Design

In the design process, we turn impossible dreams into reality. Through plans and 3D designs, we bring clients’ hopes to life, offering a glimpse of the finished project.

03

Pre-construction

In pre-production, the contractor starts work. Having the project engineer on-site is vital. Construction platforms aid seamless collaboration and scheduling.

04

Procurement

The simplest step in the manufacturing process is storage. Procurement is buying all the materials needed for the construction project that we need. In construction, that means finding workers, tools, and building materials.

05

Construction

This step has the most working parts and deadlines to coordinate. our contractor and subcontractor involved must be on time and following plans for things to work.

06

Post-construction

Everything is built. A final walkthrough check finally confirms that everything completed correctly. Then, the architect issues a substantial completion certificate.
